Where Iconic Design Meets Play:
Nike Air Max 95 x LEGO® Collection

March 18, 2026

Copy Link ↗

What to know

  • Nike and the LEGO Group are unveiling the Nike Air Max 95 x LEGO® Collection, bringing together the Air Max 95 Big Bubble sneaker and a separate LEGO brick set as the next evolution of their global partnership.
  • The release comes on the heels of Air Max Day, celebrating one of Nike’s most storied silhouettes while introducing Air Max to the next generation through creative play, imagination and self-expression.
  • Today’s announcement is part of Nike and the LEGO Group’s partnership to empower kids through the joy of active and creative play.
  • Launching globally beginning March 28, the collection blends Nike’s iconic footwear and the LEGO Group’s creative playfulness, encouraging kids to wear what they love and play all day.
