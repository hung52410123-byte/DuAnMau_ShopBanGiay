CUSTOMER SERVICE

For questions and/or concerns about products, orders, or a customer service based encounter, please contact us through our customer service channels.

At NIKE, we firmly believe in – and advocate for – a culture of respect and inclusion where everyone's voice is heard.

We urge all parties to speak up if they experience something that does not align with NIKE's values, policies or expectations.

WHO CAN REPORT?

We encourage all parties — employees and external reporters — to speak up. If you are not a NIKE employee, you are still allowed to report a concern or ask a question.

WHAT CAN I REPORT?

You can report any actual or suspected misconduct, breach, or concern relating to applicable laws and regulations, or any violation of NIKE code, policy, or strategy (to access NIKE materials select Resources).

For questions and/or concerns about products, orders, or a customer service based encounter, please contact us through our customer service channels.

Please do not use this channel to report emergencies. In case of an emergency, contact local authorities immediately.

WHAT ARE NIKE'S EXPECTATIONS?

We expect all employees and partners to comply with all applicable laws and regulations, and to meet or exceed the standards set out in our Code.

We also require our in-scope facilities and third-party logistics providers to provide safe, anonymous, confidential mechanisms for workers to report their concerns without fear of discrimination, intimidation or retaliation.

This site is provided in addition to the mechanisms outlined above. This process is not intended to replace or undermine existing internal grievance channels in factories, or legal remedies available at the country level.
